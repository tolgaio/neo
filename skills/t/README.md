# T Skills

Imported from [Fabric patterns](https://github.com/danielmiessler/Fabric/tree/main/data/patterns).

These are TELOS-based skills that analyze and provide insights based on your personal TELOS file (goals, missions, projects, challenges, etc.).

## Available Skills

| Skill | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| [analyze-challenge-handling](./analyze-challenge-handling/) | Analyzes how well you're addressing your challenges with honest feedback |
| [check-dunning-kruger](./check-dunning-kruger/) | Identifies cognitive biases where you over/underestimate competence |
| [check-metrics](./check-metrics/) | Reviews your current metrics and KPIs to see their state and progress |
| [create-h3-career](./create-h3-career/) | Explores human-centered career ideas for post-automation world |
| [create-opening-sentences](./create-opening-sentences/) | Creates humble introduction sentences about who you are and what you do |
| [describe-life-outlook](./describe-life-outlook/) | Describes your life outlook and philosophy based on your TELOS file |
| [extract-intro-sentences](./extract-intro-sentences/) | Extracts confident yet humble bio sentences about your work |
| [extract-panel-topics](./extract-panel-topics/) | Suggests panel topics ideal for you to participate in |
| [find-blindspots](./find-blindspots/) | Identifies blindspots in your thinking and mental models |
| [find-negative-thinking](./find-negative-thinking/) | Finds negative thinking patterns and provides tough love encouragement |
| [find-neglected-goals](./find-neglected-goals/) | Identifies goals and projects that haven't been worked on recently |
| [give-encouragement](./give-encouragement/) | Provides encouragement based on your progress and positive aspects |
| [red-team-thinking](./red-team-thinking/) | Red-teams your thinking and mental models with fix recommendations |
| [threat-model-plans](./threat-model-plans/) | Threat models your life plan to identify what could go wrong |
| [visualize-mission-goals-projects](./visualize-mission-goals-projects/) | Creates ASCII art diagram of missions, goals, and projects |
| [year-in-review](./year-in-review/) | Reviews yearly accomplishments with visual comparison |

## Usage

Use the `/t-{skill-name}` command to invoke a skill. For example:

```
/t-find-blindspots
/t-year-in-review
/t-give-encouragement
```

## About TELOS

These patterns expect a TELOS file as context, which contains information about:
- Your missions and purpose
- Your goals and objectives
- Current projects and work
- Challenges you're facing
- Metrics and KPIs
- Journal entries
